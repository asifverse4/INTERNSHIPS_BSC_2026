## 📌 About This Document

A comprehensive, up-to-date list of internship opportunities in India for BSc Biotechnology students and graduates (2026). Covers government research institutes, IITs/IISERs/NITs, pharma & biotech industry, and online portals.

---

## 🏛️ Government & Research Institute Internships

### 1. CSIR Labs (Council of Scientific & Industrial Research)

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology students
- **Duration:** 2–12 months
- **Stipend:** Varies by lab
- **Key Labs for Biotech:**
    - CSIR-IMTECH (Institute of Microbial Technology), Chandigarh
    - CSIR-CCMB (Centre for Cellular & Molecular Biology), Hyderabad
    - CSIR-NCL (National Chemical Laboratory), Pune
    - CSIR-IGIB (Institute of Genomics & Integrative Biology), Delhi
- **Apply:** www.csir.res.in or directly to individual lab portals

### 2. DBT (Department of Biotechnology) – Government of India

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology students
- **Programs:** JRF, SRF, and project assistant positions
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Apply:** www.dbt.gov.in

### 3. ICMR – NIIRNCD, Jodhpur Internship/Observership Programme

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Life Science/Biotechnology students
- **Duration:** 2 months (Sessions: May–June & Aug–Sept 2026)
- **Stipend:** Not specified
- **Apply:** www.icmr.gov.in

### 4. NCDC (National Centre for Disease Control), Delhi

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology, Microbiology, Life Sciences
- **Duration:** 2–6 months
- **Cycles:** January (apply Nov) & July (apply May–June)
- **Apply:** www.ncdc.mohfw.gov.in/internship

### 5. DRDO (Defence Research & Development Organisation)

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology & Life Sciences
- **Duration:** Short-term (paid scheme 2026–27)
- **Stipend:** Paid
- **Apply:** www.drdo.gov.in — Search "Paid Internship Scheme 2026"

### 6. RGCB (Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Biotechnology), Thiruvananthapuram

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology students
- **Duration:** Short-term project work
- **Stipend:** None (nominal fee may apply)
- **Apply:** www.rgcb.res.in/training

### 7. IBAB (Institute of Bioinformatics and Applied Biotechnology), Bengaluru

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology, Bioinformatics
- **Duration:** Minimum 2 months
- **Stipend:** None
- **Areas:** Biotechnology, bioinformatics, big data in biology
- **Apply:** www.ibab.ac.in/research/internship

### 8. BTM – Gujarat Biotechnology Research Centre (GBRC) SRIP

- **Eligibility:** BSc Biotechnology (2nd year / 4th semester)
- **Duration:** Summer (short-term)
- **Stipend:** Provided
- **Apply:** www.btm.gujarat.gov.in/SIPfU.htm

### 9. NCBS (National Centre for Biological Sciences), Bengaluru

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology, Molecular Biology
- **Duration:** Summer (2–3 months)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Apply:** www.Directly to faculty labs at ncbs.res.in

## 🎓 Academic / Fellowship Internships

### 10. SRFP – Summer Research Fellowship Programme (INSA / IASc / NASI)

- **Eligibility:** BSc Biotechnology (2nd, 3rd, 4th year)
- **Duration:** 2 months (summer)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Areas:** Biology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Chemistry
- **Deadline:** ~28 February (annual)
- **Apply:** www.jncasr.ac.in/srfp

### 11. TIFR – Visiting Students' Research Programme (VSRP)

- **Eligibility:** BSc Biotechnology (3rd/4th year)
- **Duration:** 2–3 months
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Areas:** Biology, Biochemistry, Molecular Biology
- **Deadline:** January–February (annual)
- **Apply:** www.tifr.res.in/~vsrp

### 12. NGSF – Next Gen Scientists Foundation Internship

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Life Sciences & Biotechnology
- **Duration:** Short-term (on-site or remote computational biology)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅ (with mentorship)
- **Apply:** www.ngsf.in

### 13. IIT Research Internships (Summer)

- **Eligibility:** BSc Biotechnology students
- **Notable IITs:** IIT Kanpur, IIT Palakkad, IIT Gandhinagar (SRIP 2026), IIT ISM Dhanbad
- **Duration:** Summer (1–2 months)
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Benefits:** Mentorship, lab access, research publications
- **Apply:** Contact faculty directly or via institute portals; gradsqr.com/summer-internships

### 14. IISER Summer Internship Programs

- **Eligibility:** BSc Biotechnology students
- **Notable IISERs:** IISER Bhopal, IISER Pune, IISER Kolkata, IISER Mohali
- **Duration:** Summer (2 months)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Apply:** Individual IISER websites (applications open Jan–Feb)

### 15. South Asian University (SAU) – Summer Research Internship 2026

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology
- **Duration:** Summer
- **Areas:** Molecular medicine, protein sciences, virology
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Apply:** www.sau.ac.in

### 16. BITP 2026 – BIBT Jaipur Biotech Internship Programme

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Biotechnology (including final year dissertation)
- **Duration:** Short to medium term
- **Focus:** Hands-on research & dissertation work
- **Apply:** www.blalbiotech.com

### 17. SRM University – AP SIF 2026

- **Eligibility:** BSc students including Biotechnology
- **Duration:** Summer
- **Stipend:** ₹8,000 ✅
- **Apply:** www.biotecnika.org

## 💡 Tips to Apply Successfully

- **Cold email professors** at IITs, IISc, IISERs — attach a short CV and Statement of Purpose
- **Apply early** — SRFP, TIFR, IISER deadlines are typically Jan–Feb every year
- **Maintain CGPA above 7.5** for competitive government fellowship programs
- **Build wet lab skills** — PCR, gel electrophoresis, cell culture, ELISA, Western blot, cloning
- **Learn bioinformatics basics** — tools like BLAST, NCBI, PyMOL, R/Python give you an edge for computational roles
- **Follow Biotecnika & Biopatrika** for daily rolling internship listings
- **PM Internship Scheme** is excellent for industry exposure with a solid government stipend
- **Consider CSIR-IMTECH or NCBS** for world-class research exposure in molecular biology
