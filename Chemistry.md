# INTERNSHIPS_BSC_CHEMISTRY



## 📌 About This Document

A comprehensive, up-to-date list of internship opportunities in India for BSc Chemistry students and graduates (2026). Covers government research institutes, IITs/IISc, pharma & industry, and online portals.

## 🏛️ Government & Research Institute Internships

### 1. CSIR-NCL (National Chemical Laboratory), Pune

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry students with excellent academic record
- **Duration:** 2–12 months
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Areas:** Chemistry, biochemistry, polymers, advanced materials
- **Apply:**  https://ncl-intern.ncl.res.in

### 2. ISRO – Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC), Thiruvananthapuram

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry students
- **Duration:** Short-term project-based
- **Stipend:** Not specified
- **Apply:**  isro.gov.in/internshipandprojects.html

### 3. ISRO – LPSC (Liquid Propulsion Systems Centre), Bengaluru

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry students only
- **Duration:** 21–45 days
- **Stipend:** No stipend
- **Apply:**  https://ipsc.gov.in/internship.html

### 4. DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation)

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry (UG/PG Science students)
- **Duration:** Short-term (paid internship scheme 2026–27)
- **Stipend:** Paid
- **Apply:**  https://drdo.gov.in — Search "Paid Internship Scheme 2026"
- **Notable Labs:** DMSRDE Kanpur, DIBT Mysuru, NMRL

### 5. Bose Institute, Kolkata

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry students
- **Duration:** Summer/short-term
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Apply:** https://boseinst.ernet.in

### 6. NISER (National Institute of Science Education and Research), Bhubaneswar

- **Eligibility:** BSc Chemistry students
- **Duration:** Summer internship
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Apply:**  www.niser.ac.in

### 7. CSIR-IMMT (Institute of Minerals and Materials Technology), Bhubaneswar

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry
- **Duration:** Short to medium term
- **Apply:**  www.immt.res.in

### 8. MoEF (Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change) Internship

- **Eligibility:** Chemical Science graduates
- **Duration:** Summer 2026
- **Stipend:** Government stipend provided
- **Apply:**  www.rasayanika.com – MoEF Internship 2026

## 🎓 Academic / Fellowship Internships

### 9. SRFP – Summer Research Fellowship Programme (INSA / IASc / NASI / JNCASR)

- **Eligibility:** BSc Chemistry (2nd, 3rd, 4th year)
- **Duration:** 2 months (summer)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Areas:** Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Maths
- **Deadline:** ~28 February (annual)
- **Apply:**  www.jncasr.ac.in/srfp

### 10. TIFR – Visiting Students' Research Programme (VSRP)

- **Eligibility:** BSc Chemistry (3rd/4th year)
- **Duration:** 2–3 months
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Areas:** Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Maths
- **Deadline:** January–February (annual)
- **Apply:**  www.tifr.res.in/~vsrp

### 11. ICTS-TIFR – S. N. Bhatt Memorial Excellence Fellowship

- **Eligibility:** BSc Chemistry (2nd–5th year)
- **Duration:** 2 months
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Deadline:** ~7 January (annual)
- **Apply:**  www.icts.res.in

### 12. IIT Mandi – Summer Internship

- **Eligibility:** BSc Chemistry students
- **Duration:** Summer
- **Stipend:** Varies
- **Apply:**  Directly contact faculty via iitmandi.ac.in

### 13. NIPER Hyderabad – Summer Internship

- **Eligibility:** BSc/MSc Chemistry students
- **Duration:** Summer
- **Areas:** Medicinal Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Analysis, Pharmacology
- **Apply:**  www.niperhyd.ac.in/summerinternship.html

### 14. HBCSE-TIFR – NIUS (National Initiative on Undergraduate Science)

- **Eligibility:** 1st year BSc students
- **Duration:** 2 years (intensive program)
- **Stipend:** Funded ✅
- **Apply:**  www.hbcse.tifr.res.in

## 💡 Tips to Apply Successfully

- **Cold email professors** at IITs, IISc, IISER — attach a brief CV and SOP
- **Apply early** — SRFP, TIFR, ICTS deadlines are Jan–Feb every year
- **Keep your CGPA above 7.5** for most competitive programs
- **Learn lab skills** — Spectroscopy (UV-Vis, FTIR, NMR), HPLC, titration
- **Mention software skills** — ChemDraw, Origin, MS Excel for data analysis
- **Check Rasayanika weekly** for rolling internship listings
- **PM Internship Scheme** is a great government-backed opportunity with a solid stipend
